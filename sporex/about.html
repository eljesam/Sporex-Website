About SporeX

We're building smarter tools to detect mould early, protect your health, and create safer indoor environments.

Our Mission

Mould is often invisible until it becomes a serious problem — impacting health, damaging homes, and increasing costs.

SporeX exists to give people early visibility into mould risks so they can act before it spreads.

How SporeX Started

September

The Problem

We saw how often mould goes undetected until it becomes a serious issue.

December

Early Development

We started building a system to monitor environmental conditions linked to mould growth.

February

Testing

Prototypes were tested in real homes to improve detection accuracy.

March

Launch

SporeX launches to help people prevent mould before it becomes a problem.

Why It Matters

Health Risks

Mould exposure can trigger asthma and respiratory conditions.

Hidden Growth

Mould often develops out of sight before becoming visible.

Costly Damage

Late detection can result in expensive repairs.

Our Approach

SporeX monitors environmental conditions and detects patterns that indicate mould risk.

  • ✔ Continuous monitoring
  • ✔ Early risk detection
  • ✔ Smart alerts
