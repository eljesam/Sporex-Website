SporeX Device Guide

Learn how the SporeX device works and how to connect it to your app.

About the SporeX Device

The SporeX device is a smart environmental monitor designed to detect conditions that lead to mould growth. It continuously tracks humidity, temperature, and air quality to identify potential risks before mould becomes visible.

  • ✔ Real-time environmental monitoring
  • ✔ Mould risk detection algorithms
  • ✔ Wireless connectivity to mobile app

How It Works

SporeX analyses environmental patterns that typically lead to mould formation. When conditions become risky, the system alerts you through the app so you can take action early.

1. Monitor

Tracks humidity and temperature continuously.

2. Analyse

Detects patterns linked to mould growth.

3. Alert

Sends notifications when risk increases.

How to Connect Your Device

1

Download the SporeX App

Install the app from the App Store or Google Play.

2

Power On the Device

Plug in your SporeX device and ensure it is powered on.

3

Connect via Wi-Fi

Open the app and follow the instructions to connect your device to your Wi-Fi network.

4

Pair Your Device

Select your device in the app and complete the pairing process.

5

Start Monitoring

Once connected, your device will begin tracking your environment automatically.

Troubleshooting

Device not connecting?
Make sure your Wi-Fi is 2.4GHz and your phone is on the same network.

No data showing?
Check that the device is powered and within range.

App not detecting device?
Restart the device and try pairing again.
